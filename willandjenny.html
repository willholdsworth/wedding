GETTING TO THE VENUE

After a short rip around the country lanes you'll find a parking area across the road from the main entrance to the Henry Moore Studio and Gardens. From there it's just a short walk down to the Aisled Barn where the ceremony and reception will be taking place.

DETAILS FOR THE DAY

What time do I need to get there?

Guests can arrive any time after 2.15pm. The ceremony will take place at 3pm. Drinks and canapes will follow after the ceremony at around 3.30pm.

What is the dress code?

Relaxed formalwear will be perfect for the day. 

(So long as it isn't a plain white dress you'll be fine). 

I'd like to book into a hotel

We'll be staying nearby in the Manor Of Groves hotel which you are welcome to stay in. We've reserved rooms if you'd like to stay the night. There's a code to quote when booking (GA001489) that applies Saturday into Sunday. There are also two Premier Inn's located in Ware or Harlow North that you're also welcome to book into if you'd prefer.

Transport on the day

We've arranged carriages at midnight to take guests back to the Manor Of Groves hotel just before midnight. There is also a local taxi company called ... if you'd like to arrange your own transport to and from the venue. There is on-site parking if you'd like to drive yourself on the day.

Wedding Gifts?

All we hope for is to celebrate our wedding with you. However, if you do choose to bring a gift it will be greatly appreciated.
